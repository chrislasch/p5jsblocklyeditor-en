Tutorial: The First Program

Create Canvas

  • Delete all existing blocks by dragging the existing blocks to the toolbar or trash.

  • The "setup()" block must be present in every program.

    Drag the "setup()" block from the "p5-Functions" category into the editor. Enter the desired width (e.g., 300) and height (e.g., 400) of the canvas in pixels.

  • The drawing area should be colored with a color.

    Drag the top "Background Color" block from the "Appearance" category into the editor and choose a color by clicking on the color field behind "Background Color".

  • Click on the "Execute Code" button and a canvas in your chosen size and color will be created.
