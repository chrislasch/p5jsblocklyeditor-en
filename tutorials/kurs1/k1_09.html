    You observe something unexpected: the circle is not only scaled but also moved.

  • The reason for the simultaneous scaling and translation is that the entire drawing area is scaled. This also makes distances larger. The circle was placed at coordinates (10/10) and had a small distance from the origin in the upper left corner at the beginning. With scaling, this distance is increased, which is why the circle is moved down and to the right.
