  • In a program, two turtles can be on the move. To do this, you define two turtle variables (e.g. "turtle1" and "turtle2") and insert them into the program.

    For both turtles, you add the "Record Movement" blocks at the end of "setup()". For recording the movement, you define two more variables, e.g. "turtleRun1" and "turtleRun2".

    In "draw()" you add the "Start Animation" block for both turtles. The speeds can be different for both turtles and the line thickness can also be set differently for both turtles.

    Now you teach each turtle what it should do. In each turtle block, you must specify the desired turtle variable (e.g. "turtle1" or "turtle2").
